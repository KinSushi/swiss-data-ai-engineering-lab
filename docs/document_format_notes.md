# Business document format notes

In finance, analysts and engineers often receive PDF, DOCX, XLSX and PPTX files. This repository does not commit private office documents. It documents their role only:

- PDF: reports, policies, contracts, regulatory documents.
- DOCX: business specifications and procedure documents.
- XLSX: operational reporting and reconciliation workbooks.
- PPTX: steering committees and project governance decks.

For public GitHub, prefer Markdown and sanitized SVG diagrams.
